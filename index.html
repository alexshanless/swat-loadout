About D.A.G.R.E

DAGRE is a Ready or Not milsim community dedicated to teaching and applying real-world TTPs (Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures) in tactical operations. We offer expert Ready or Not training, structured deployments, and raids that help you master the game. We proudly welcome all brothers and sisters who choose to join our ranks and become part of one of the gaming community's finest Ready or Not organizations.

At DAGRE, we value genuine connection. Our mission is to enhance your Ready or Not gameplay and tactical skills while building a positive, motivating community that uplifts members both in-game and in their day-to-day lives.
